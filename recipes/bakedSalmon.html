Baked Salmon

Baked salmon is a dish enjoyed all around the world. It is very healthy and filled with nutrients and is also a good source of protein. There are a few ways to make it but this recipe focuses on baking the salmon. Continue reading to see how it's made.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound side of salmon
  • 5 springs of fresh rosemary
  • 2 small lemons
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • Chopped herbs (basil, thyme, parsley, dill, green onion)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F and thaw out salmon while preparing other ingredients
  2. Lightly coat alumninum foil with baking spray and put 2 springs of rosemary down the middle
  3. Drizzle salmon with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper and other seasonings of choice
  4. Fold foil over salmon and bake in over for 15-20 minutes
  5. Open foil and set the over to broil and leave salmon for 3 minutes
  6. Cut salmon into portions and serve as desired
